RhinoCommon to COMPAS object conversions and other utilities

Scripts for converting geometry objects between the two frameworks. Most of this has been integrated into COMPAS v0.15.0 but compas_fab needs earlier versions of COMPAS.

Points

cgpoint_to_rgpoint(pt)

Convert compas.geometry.Point to Rhino.Geometry.Point3d.

rgpoint_to_cgpoint(pt)

Convert Rhino.Geometry.Point3d to compas.geometry.Point.

Vectors

cgvector_to_rgvector(v)

Convert compas.geometry.Vector to Rhino.Geometry.Vector3d.

rgvector_to_cgvector(v)

Convert Rhino.Geometry.Vector3d to compas.geometry.Vector.

Lines

cgline_to_rgline(line)

Convert compas.geometry.Line to Rhino.Geometry.Line.

rgline_to_cgline(line)

Convert Rhino.Geometry.Line to compas.geometry.Line.

Planes & Frames

cgplane_to_rgplane(cgplane)

Convert compas.geometry.Plane to Rhino.Geometry.Plane.

rgplane_to_cgplane(plane)

Convert Rhino.Geometry.Plane to compas.geometry.Plane.

rgplane_to_cgframe(plane)

Convert Rhino.Geometry.Plane to compas.geometry.Frame.

Transformations

matrix_to_rgtransform(M)

Create Rhino.Geometry.Transform from a transformation matrix.

rgtransform_to_matrix(rgM)

Convert Rhino.Geometry.Transform to transformation matrix.
